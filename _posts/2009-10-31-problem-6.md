The [problem 6](http://projecteuler.net/index.php?section=problems&id=6) of
the Euler project site was again easy to solve. Using Haskell, I wrote the
following one-line function: 

{% highlight haskell %}
    g = (sum [1..100] ^ 2) - (sum (map (^2) [1..100]))
{% endhighlight %}
